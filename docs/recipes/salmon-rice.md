# Salmon Rice Bowl

VERY easy to make high protein meal. 

## Ingredients

- ? oz can salmon
- 1 tbsp kewpie mayo
- 2 scallions / green onions
- 2 tsp sesame oil
- pinch salt
- as much furikake as desired
- 1/2 cup cucumber (optional)
- 2 tbsp hot sauce (optional)

## Directions
1. combine ingredients in large bowl
2. eat with rice or however you wish
