<p><em>The 7-Minute CHOP Workout (POTS-Friendly Version). Each exercise is done for 30 seconds, followed by a 10-second rest:</em></p>
<table>
<tr>
    <td>1.♈ <em>Wall Sit</em>: Sit with your back against a wall, knees bent at a 90-degree angle.</td>
  </tr>
<tr>
    <td>2.♉ <em>Chair-Assisted Push-Ups</em>: Use a sturdy chair to perform push-ups, either standing or kneeling.</td>
</tr>
    <tr>
    <td>3.♊ <em>Seated Marching</em>: While sitting, lift your knees alternately to engage your core and leg muscles without standing up.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>4.♋ <em>Seated Leg Raises</em>: While seated, extend one leg at a time, engaging the thigh muscles.</td>
  </tr>
<tr>
    <td>5.♌ <em>Chair-Assisted Squats</em>: Stand up from a seated position using a chair for support, then sit back down.</td>
</tr>
    <tr>
    <td>6.♍ <em>Wall Push-Ups</em>: Stand facing a wall, place your hands on the wall, and perform push-ups by leaning into it.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>7.♎ <em>Seated Shoulder Press (using light resistance bands or body weight)</em>: Sit and press your arms overhead as though lifting weights.</td>
  </tr>
<tr>
    <td>8.♏ <em>Seated Side Leg Lifts</em>: While sitting, lift one leg out to the side, then switch.</td>
</tr>
    <tr>
    <td>9.♐ <em>Seated Toe Taps</em>: Tap your toes in front of you while seated, engaging your leg and core muscles.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
    <td>10.♑ <em>Chair-Assisted Step-Ups</em>: Using a low step or sturdy platform, step up and down, holding onto a chair or railing for balance.</td>
  </tr>
<tr>
    <td>11.♒ <em>Seated Side Twists</em>: While sitting, twist your upper body from side to side, engaging the core.</td>
</tr>
    <tr>
    <td>12.♓ <em>Plank (Modified to Kneeling)</em>: Perform a plank from your knees, keeping your core engaged.</td>
</tr>
</table>
